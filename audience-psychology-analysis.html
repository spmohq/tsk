Audience & Content Analysis Prompts

Use the prompts below to perform a deep analysis of any text.

Prompt 1: Audience Psychology Analysis

Read the text below and answer the following questions about its intended audience.
Please provide your answers in a clear, structured format (bullet points or numbered lists):

1. What fears of the readers does this content address?
2. What misunderstandings, ignorance, or distrust of the readers does this content address?
3. What doubts of the readers does this content address?
4. What limitations or inabilities of the readers does this content address?

Finally, provide a 2–3 sentence summary insight of what this analysis reveals about the intended audience.

Prompt 2: Content Analysis

Using the same text (repeated below for reference), answer the following questions.
Please provide your answers in a clear, structured format (bullet points or numbered lists):

1. A bullet list of the logical arguments this content uses
2. Any data (facts, statistics, or figures) this content uses
3. Any reports, studies, or research cited
4. Anecdotes or personal accounts included
5. Examples used
6. Stories or narratives included
7. Counterarguments addressed
8. Possible solutions or recommendations provided

Finally, provide a 2–3 sentence summary insight of what this analysis reveals about the text’s overall persuasiveness and strategy.

Prompt 3: Synthesis & Alignment

Based on the insights from Prompt 1 (audience analysis) and Prompt 2 (content analysis), please provide:

1. How well the content addresses the audience’s fears, doubts, misunderstandings, and limitations
2. Any mismatches between what the audience needs and what the content provides
3. Strengths in the alignment between content and audience
4. Weaknesses or gaps where the content could better address the audience

Finally, provide a concise 3–4 sentence synthesis insight that explains how effectively this text connects with its readers and what could improve its impact.

Prompt 4: Improvement & Rewriting

Using the insights from Prompts 1–3, please improve the original text so it better resonates with its intended audience. Specifically:

1. Strengthen how it addresses audience fears, doubts, misunderstandings, and limitations
2. Fill any gaps in logic, data, stories, or counterarguments that were missing
3. Enhance its persuasiveness and clarity while keeping the original intent intact
4. Write the improved version in a clear, engaging style suitable for the audience

Finally, provide a short note explaining what key improvements you made and why.
